Codes of Conduct

  1. Maintain confidentiality of client and company information.
  2. Adhere to all cybersecurity protocols and best practices.
  3. Respect intellectual property rights and avoid plagiarism.
  4. Ensure all software is tested and free of vulnerabilities.
  5. Provide accurate and honest communication with clients.
  6. Report any security breaches or incidents immediately.
  7. Follow ethical guidelines in all business dealings.
  8. Continuously update skills and knowledge in the IT field.
  9. Promote a positive and inclusive work environment.
  10. Comply with all UK laws and regulations.
